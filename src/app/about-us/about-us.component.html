About Us

Our History

Founded in 1995, ThaiAir has been committed to providing exceptional travel experiences to passengers around the world. Over the past decades, we have expanded our fleet, introduced new destinations, and continuously enhanced our services to ensure our customers have a comfortable and enjoyable journey.

Our airline started with a vision to connect people and places, and today, we proudly operate flights to over 100 destinations across the globe. Our dedication to safety, reliability, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted choice for millions of travelers.

Our Mission & Values

At ThaiAir, our mission is to make air travel accessible, enjoyable, and memorable for everyone. We are guided by our core values:

  • Safety: Ensuring the highest standards of safety for our passengers and crew.
  • Customer Focus: Putting our customers at the heart of everything we do and striving to exceed their expectations.
  • Integrity: Conducting our business with honesty, transparency, and accountability.
  • Innovation: Embracing innovation to enhance our services and create a seamless travel experience.
  • Sustainability: Committing to sustainable practices to protect our planet for future generations.
